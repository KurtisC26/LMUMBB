LMUMBB Game Stats Tracker

Stat Tracker Guide

Usage guide:

This web application is a tool to help track internal metrics, and attribute lineup combinations in order to produce a custom play-by-play data set. Users can choose to implement as few or as many of the variables. Variables you choose not to track should be left blank.

It is important to note that the data is not linked to a database(yet), so refreshing the URL during a tracking session will cause the loss of data.

Once the possession is complete, press the "Submit" button to log the data in the live data table.

Once the session is complete, click on the "Export to CSV" button to download the data and analyze.

Variable Definitions:

  • Opponent: Team or session type for the events
  • Players (P1 thru P5): Lineup that is on the court during the events
  • Possession Type (Pos Type): Offensive or defensive possession
  • Possession Start (Pos Start): The type of situation that initiated the possession
  • Play Developement: Executed play type sequece during the possession
  • Play Movement: Generalized description of the path taken by the ball during the possession
  • Shot Selection: Type of shot attempted
  • Shot Location General location of the shot attempted
  • Play Result: Result of the actions executed during the possession
  • Play Result: Result of the actions executed during the possession
  • Points: points scored or allowed during the possession
  • Player with action: Player who attempts the shot on offense, or who is attempting to challenge the shot on defense
